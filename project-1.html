Insight

We live in a culture where the best experiences are often singular. Theme parks are the pinnacle of this frustration: you wait in line for a long time — for a short thrill.

Twix has always been about the double joy and if one bar is never enough, one ride shouldn’t be either.

Solution / Idea

Twix hijacks Groundhog Day to turn the “unsharable” nature of Twix into the most sharable experience in Orlando. A total theme park takeover where everything, from the rides to the prizes comes in pairs.
